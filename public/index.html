How to Use Fiction Fusion

  1. Choose a model on the right: GPT-3, NEO, or GPT-J. We recommend trying GPT-3 first as it has the best overall performance.
  2. Start writing the first sentence of your story.
  3. When you finish the sentence and end it with a period (.), the AI will start writing the next sentence after a delay of 3 seconds.
  4. Continue writing and collaborating with the AI co-author line by line.
  5. Note that the AI supports the Dutch language, but it is more creative and proficient in English.
  6. Click here to share your experience! It will only take 2 minutes.

Tip: the AI might be steered in certain directions as it predicts the possible context and your intent at every new sentence.
